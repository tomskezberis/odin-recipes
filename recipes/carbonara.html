Carbonara

Carbonara (Italian: [karboˈnaːra]) is a pasta dish made with eggs, hard cheese, fatty cured pork, and black pepper.

The dish took its modern form and name in the middle of the 20th century. The cheese is usually pecorino romano.

Ingredients

  • Egg yolk
  • Pork fatty
  • Hard cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta
  2. Cook the pork fat on the pan till it turns out crunchy
  3. Add pasta to pork fat and mix in egg yolk
