Pasta Carbonara

Description

Pasta Carbonara is a classic Roman dish made with spaghetti, guanciale (cured pork jowl), eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, and black pepper

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Guanciale
  • Spaghetti
  • Pecorino cheese
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta in salted boiling water until al dente, reserving at least 1 cup of pasta water
  2. Fry pancetta, guanciale, or bacon in a skillet over medium heat until crisp, then remove and set aside
  3. Whisk eggs, grated Pecorino Romano and Parmesan cheese, and a generous amount of black pepper in a bowl
  4. Remove the skillet from heat, add the hot pasta, and quickly stir in the egg mixture while tossing continuously
  5. Add reserved pasta water (1–2 tablespoons at a time) to create a silky, emulsified sauce
  6. Finish by mixing in the crispy pork and serving immediately with extra cheese and pepper
