Pasta Vongole

Description

Pasta alle vongole is a classic Italian seafood pasta dish featuring fresh clams (vongole) cooked with garlic, olive oil, parsley, and white wine.

Ingredients

  • Fresh clams (vongole)
  • Pasta: Traditionally spaghetti or linguine
  • Garlic: Freshly sliced or chopped
  • Extra virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. lean and Prep Clams: Soak fresh vongole in salted water (3% salinity) for 2–4 hours to purge sand. Discard any clams that remain open or don’t close when tapped.
  2. Sauté Aromatics: In a large pan, heat extra virgin olive oil over medium-low heat. Add minced garlic and chopped parsley stems (or whole garlic cloves), and cook gently until fragrant
  3. Cook Clams: Add the cleaned vongole to the pan, stir to coat in oil, then pour in white wine. Cover with a lid or foil and cook for 5–7 minutes, shaking the pan occasionally, until clams open
  4. Cook Pasta and Combine: Boil spaghetti in heavily salted water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water. Drain pasta, then immediately toss it into the clam pan with the sauce.
